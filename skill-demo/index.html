timeseries-radar-3d skill demo preview

Dependency-free canvas build of the timeseries-radar-3d skill (no Three.js). Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, click a slice for its flat view, and click an axis (or a row in "Where you stand") to drill into its submetrics and sources. Synthetic sample data. This page is a temporary public preview of the skill's own demo.

Drop-in: ScanRadar3D.mount(el, { dataUrl: "timeline.json" }).
